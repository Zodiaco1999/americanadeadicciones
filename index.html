About Us

We are an entity that seeks to lead the work by promoting and recognizing the rights of children, adolescents and their families. At the American Foundation of Addictions we work hard to prevent drug use in vulnerable populations; For this we have linked to the private sector, public sector and national and international organizations.

Executive Team

Patricia Santos

Founder & CEO

Master's Degree in the Treatment of Addictive Behaviors from the University of Valencia in Spain; Psychologist graduated from the Konrad Lorenz University Foundation, Lecturer and workshop leader in neuropsychology in Latin America and the EEUU, she has more than 10 years of experience in modifying disruptive behaviors against the use of psychoactive substances and behavior modification in children and adolescents.

Creator of the selective prevention program based on scientific evidence Young people without drugs Parents With Rules, implemented in public and private educational institutions, co-author of the “Be Productive” Mental Health at Work Program which seeks to advise and train organizations in construction of its internal alcohol and drug policies, it is currently conducting an interdisciplinary investigation whose objective is to bring together the fields of public health, criminology and criminal justice, focused on the prevention of drug use and trafficking in the Americas.

Luis Maldonado

Director of Academic Programs

Career educator in public education in Bogotá D.C., PhD in Education from the Universidad del Valle. Researcher of the relationship of Latin American regional integration processes and their relationship with education and culture. Permanent reader of humanities, literature, cosmology and biology.
