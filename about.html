About Us

Today, the climate crisis poses a threat to the progress and well-being of millions of families. Despite existing innovations, the transformation needed to address this crisis and promote equity, We believe that family unity and that they deserve the best By accelerating and ensuring that everyone can participate in the transformations that are underway in our welfare system.

Mission

Improve the quality of life of children, adolescents and their families in a condition of vulnerability due to the use of psychoactive substances or any type of violence, developing psychological and educational programs that promote mental health and generate opportunities for changes and resocialization of Beneficiary population. Prevent, educate, rehabilitate and reinsert the population about the consumption of psychoactive substances and other risk behaviors that impede or hinder the integral development of adolescent children and their families in America.

Vision

The American Addictions Foundation will be recognized as a leading entity in developing educational impact programs, seeking socio-emotional and resilient transformation in children, adolescents, adolescents in conflict with the law, persons deprived of liberty and their families.

Goals

School Prevention: Our main objective is to prevent the consumption of psychoactive substances in children and adolescents from an early age, developing programs of high social impact in schools based on scientific evidence, mental health and healthy lifestyle habits.

Young people in conflict with the law: Visualize adolescents in conflict with the law prosecuted by the System of Criminal Responsibility for Adolescents, guiding them and educating them in a life project away from crime, seeking their reintegration into society as agents of change.

Persons deprived of liberty: We work to reduce the consumption of psychoactive substances within prisons, we are addiction specialists with extensive international knowledge in psychosocial and educational intervention.
